About

What is Magnolia?

Based in Chapel Hill, we focus on the development of smart traffic lights. By allowing computer vision to assess each intersection, we're able to reduce traffic congestion through optimized signal timings. We then allow each intersection to share this data with one another, creating a grid of smart intersections with optimized routes for our drivers.

Mission Statement

What we do

Magnolia optimizes traffic flow through the use of our specialized cameras real-time data. We aim to make downtown congestion a thing of the past, so that everyone in town can get where they need to be.
